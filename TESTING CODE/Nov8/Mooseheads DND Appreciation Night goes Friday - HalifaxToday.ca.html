Mooseheads DND Appreciation Night goes Friday

The team will wear special Shearwater 100 jerseys that will be raffled off during the game
5
about an hour ago by: HalifaxToday Staff
(Contributed)

NEWS RELEASE
HALIFAX MOOSEHEADS
*************************
The Halifax Mooseheads will host their 15th Annual DND Appreciation Night this Friday at 7pm versus the Cape Breton Screaming Eagles.

This year, to honour 12 Wing Shearwater on its 100th anniversary, the team will wear special Shearwater 100 jerseys that will be raffled off during the game. Funds raised through the jersey raffle go to the Camp Hill Veterans' Memorial Garden at the QEII Health Sciences Centre.

“This is always an important game for our Organization” stated Brian Urquhart, President Halifax Mooseheads. “We have a great partnership with the men and women of our Canadian Forces and this is our chance, along with our fans, to say thanks for the sacrifices they make for our Country.”

“Our connection with the Halifax Mooseheads is strong and unique,” stated Capt(N) Dave Mazur, Base Commander, CFB Halifax.“The military shares many positive core values and it makes for a great relationship. We appreciate their friendship and kind gestures”, he added.

Tickets are on sale now at the Ticket Atlantic Box Office, select Superstore Outlets, TicketAtlantic.com, the Official Mooseheads Shop in Scotia Square or charge by phone 902-451-1221.Specially priced DND tickets can be purchased at any PSP Fitness, Sports and Recreation centre.
*************************
